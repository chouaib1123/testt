Answers to Common Inquiries
Is it possible to promote a product that is available in a different country?
Only products available in the same country are promoted. For example, a product that is only available in Senegal cannot be sold in Côte d’Ivoire, Mali or any other country.
What is the minimum amount that can be withdrawn?
The minimum payment of dues is $50 for all states.
What are the conditions for accepting my account?
All information must be correct and the national identity card or passport must be uploaded.
How are requests for offers accepted?
Requests for offers are accepted according to the available quantity of the product that is supplied periodically.
Are there ways to market instead of a referral link?
Instead of referral links, we offer several platforms and solutions: Yokan platform, WooCommerce Shopify. (All link explanations are on our YouTube channel).
What payment methods are available?
Available payment methods are: Paypal Payoneer TransferWise Bank Account
Is it possible to work in one yokan-woocommerce store in all countries?
It is preferable to create a store for each country because the store currency must match the display currency.
